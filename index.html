Florida Bird Identification Game


About This Game

Welcome to the Tampa Bay Bird Identification Game! This game is designed to help you sharpen your skills in recognizing birds by sight. Whether you're a beginner birder, a biologist preparing for an upcoming survey, or anyone looking for a great way to impress their friends at a party, this game is for you.

You'll be presented with a series of images and your goal is to correctly identify the species from a list of choices. After each round, you can review your answers and see how you did. Your objective is to get the highest score!

Good luck!
